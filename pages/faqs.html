Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I don't like the default view of BlueMap, is there any better way to view it?

A: YES!! If you're on PC, there's 3 icons in the top right that you can click to change the vieing mode. On mobile, click the 3 lines in the top left, then settings, and under View / Controls (should be at the top) you can select your viewing mode.

The 3 viewing modes are persepctive, flat, and free-flight, free flight is similar to playing in spectator mode, so you should use that if you want to see the most details.

Q: How do I change dimensions in BlueMap

A: If you click the 3 lines in the top left, then "Maps" You can select the dimension you'd like to view.

Q: Why does the 1.12.2 modded server (Mpreg Server (feat. Darrius)) world have a different looking map than the rest of the worlds?

A: While bluemap technically has support for modded blocks below version 1.13, it's an incredible hastle, you have to manually add every texture and block id, which would amount to thousands of hours of work (on top of the hundreds of hours already spent on this project).

On the other hand, Dynmap has support to automatically scan all jar files and extract textures and item ids automatically. Even with that, there's still a few blocks that Dynmap couldn't figure out how to display. However, it was a much more reliable solution than BlueMap for a pre-1.13 modded server.

Q: How do you change perspectives / dimensions in Dynmap?

A: On the left side of the screen, there is a thin line that spans the height of the screen with an arrow in the middle. If you hover over (or tap on mobile) this line, you will be shown a list of maps that you can view, including multiple dimensions and perspectives.
